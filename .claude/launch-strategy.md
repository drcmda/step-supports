# Launch Strategy

## Timing
- **Best day:** Tuesday or Wednesday
- **Best time:** 9-10am ET / 6-7am PT (US morning + European afternoon)
- **Avoid:** launching on major Bambu/Prusa announcement days

## Platform order

### 1. Reddit (first, highest ROI)
- r/3Dprinting (7M+) — main audience
- r/functionalprint
- r/ender3, r/BambuLab — slicer-adjacent
- Frame as "I built X" with before/after photo or GIF of supports snapping off

### 2. Hacker News (same day)
- "Show HN: Negative-space 3D print supports — generated automatically"
- Boolean-subtraction algorithm angle appeals to HN technical crowd
- Post 8-9am ET

### 3. Twitter/X (same day)
- Short video: model -> supports -> snap-off
- Tag @BambuLab, @Prusa3D, @thanabordeaux

### 4. YouTube (optional, day before or same day)
- 60-90 second demo, link in all other posts

## What performs best
- 10-second video of supports snapping off cleanly
- Before/after comparison: tree supports scarring vs. negative-space clean finish
- "I made a tool that..." framing, not "announcing my product"
